CARHARTT

WHO WE ARE

Carhartt is an American company specializing in the production of durable workwear and casual clothing. Founded in 1889 in Detroit, Michigan, by Hamilton Carhartt, the company is known for its quality and durability of products, which are often made of sturdy materials such as denim and canvas. Carhartt has earned a reputation for its strength and durability, making it a popular choice among manual workers, farmers, construction workers and outdoor enthusiasts. The brand offers a wide range of clothing, including jackets, trousers, shirts, tracksuits and accessories. In recent years, Carhartt has also gained considerable popularity in streetwear fashion, as its rustic style has become a popular trend among many young people. The company has managed to combine its heritage in workwear with a modern aesthetic, making it a versatile fashion choice for a wide range of consumers.

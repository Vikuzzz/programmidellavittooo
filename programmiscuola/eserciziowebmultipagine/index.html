January 1, 2014 by Mark

In 1889, our founder, Hamilton Carhartt, firmly believed that "when I make a sale I make a lasting friend." A belief that continues to live in the more than 5,000 members of the Carhartt family.

Here's how they put their Carhartt gear to work: from rugged, breathable work pants to sweat-fighting Force T-shirts, from comfy cotton hoodies to work-ready leggings.

So today we want to say "Thank You" to our friends who work in the heat, the cold and the rain. And who have never stopped working in difficult and uncertain times.

Carhartt technologies

The Rain Defender™ lining in our Carhartt apparel is the result of water-repellent technology, meaning our Rain Defender™ jackets are built to withstand light rain, on the job and beyond.

  • Rain Defender;
  • Storm Defender;
  • Super Dux;
  • Carhartt Force;
  • Rugged Flex.

The waterproof and breathable Storm Defender™ outdoor clothing prevents the penetration of every single drop of rain while allowing body heat to escape.
